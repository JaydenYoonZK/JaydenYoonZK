<b>Jayden Yoon Zeng Khai</b> <i>(Traditional Chinese: 袁正凱; Simplified Chinese: 袁正凯; Pinyin: Yuán Zhèng Kǎi)</i> was born on 20th of March, 1997, in Kota Kinabalu, Sabah, Malaysia. A Malaysian-Chinese entrepreneur, philanthropist, investor, singer/songwriter, musician, composer, producer, director, writer, actor, designer, photographer, programmer, dancer, beat-boxer, best known as an ARTist and the Founder & CEO of JaydenART® Inc., based in California, USA.
